About Me

Fintech | BizOps | Product Mgmt

I have a deep-rooted belief in the transformative power of technology and entrepreneurship to solve big problems, in particular across the African continent. My affinity for technology-centric innovation stems from my academic studies in Information Systems. I enjoy first-principles thinking and breaking down complex systems/problems into simple components.

I am extremely process-oriented and thrive in chaotic environments that require unpacking systems/workflows and building efficient knowledge repositories in logical, structure approach. I am passionately collaborative. What gets me up in the morning is the opportunity to contribute to a team/plan coming together under a common goal. I am impact oriented; when I have a problem, I am creative and tenacious about solving it
